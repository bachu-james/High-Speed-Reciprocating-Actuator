The motor approaches the hard-stop at 0.1 rev/s — slow enough that kinetic energy at impact is negligible, so mechanical bounce is not a practical concern. When q_current exceeds the 0.3A threshold, set_stop() is called immediately to de-energise the motor instead of holding a position command against the wall, which would cause sustained high current.

The home offset is recorded after a short 100ms settle delay, not at the exact moment of threshold crossing, to absorb any residual motion before the encoder value is captured as Position 0. All subsequent move targets are expressed relative to this offset, so any small positioning error at homing cancels out consistently across cycles.
